Every payment, screened

Five signals, weighed in an instant

The moment you hit pay, WallUPI quietly reads the situation, not just the amount.

Who you're paying

Is this someone you've paid before, or a brand-new payee? Has the community flagged this UPI ID?

Is the amount normal

A sudden, out-of-character transfer to a new contact stands out, even when everything else looks fine.

Are you on a call

Scammers keep you talking so you can't think. An active call during payment raises the alarm.

A suspicious SMS

If a scam-like message recently mentioned this recipient, WallUPI treats it as a serious warning.

How you got here

Did you scan a QR or follow a link? Those entry points carry more risk than typing an ID yourself.

One clear verdict

All the signals come together into a single, easy-to-read risk read-out, so the decision is yours and fully informed.

Under the hood

Three layers of AI, working together

They run side by side on every message and payment: fast, private, and always on.

1
On-device · always on

Instant rule engine

A lightning-fast first pass that runs entirely on your phone, with no internet needed. It catches the obvious red flags the instant they appear.

2
On-device ML · private

TinyBERT on your phone

A compact language model reads messages locally and sorts them into scam categories: phishing, fake KYC, OTP theft and more. Fast, offline, and your data never leaves the device.

Cloud second opinion

A larger model, when it counts

When something looks borderline, a sanitized, anonymous snippet is sent to a powerful cloud model for a closer look, returning a plain-English explanation of what it found and why.

Frequently asked

What exactly is WallUPI?

A UPI payment app with a scam check built in. Every time you pay, it reads the situation: the recipient, the amount, your recent messages and whether you're on a call, then steps in before the money leaves your account. It's a demonstration app, so no real money moves, but the detection underneath is real.

How does it catch a scam I can't see?

It weighs five signals at once: who you're paying, whether the amount is unusual for you, whether you're on a call right now, whether a scam-like SMS recently mentioned this number, and how you reached the payment screen. Together they produce a single risk score from 0 to 100.

Will it block my payment, or just warn me?

It depends on the score. Low risk goes straight through. Medium risk shows a quick review card. High risk asks you to confirm you understand the warning. Critical risk pauses the payment with a forced cooldown, enough time to break the pressure and think again.

It reads my SMS. Is that private?

Yes. Messages are analysed on your device, not in the cloud. Only when something looks genuinely borderline is an anonymised snippet sent for a second opinion, with names, numbers and account details removed first.

Does it need an internet connection?

No. The on-device rule engine and the TinyBERT model handle the everyday checks completely offline. The cloud model is only consulted for the rare borderline case.

How is this different from my current UPI app?

Most apps move the money the instant you confirm. WallUPI adds a safety layer that looks for fraud first, so a scam is caught before you tap send, not reported after the money's already gone.
