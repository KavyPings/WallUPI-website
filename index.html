Under the hood

Three layers of AI, working together

They run side by side on every message and payment — fast, private, and always on.

1
On-device · always on

Instant rule engine

A lightning-fast first pass that runs entirely on your phone, with no internet needed. It catches the obvious red flags the instant they appear.

2
On-device ML · private

TinyBERT on your phone

A compact language model reads messages locally and sorts them into scam categories — phishing, fake KYC, OTP theft and more. Fast, offline, and your data never leaves the device.

3
Cloud second opinion

A larger model, when it counts

When something looks borderline, a sanitized, anonymous snippet is sent to a powerful cloud model for a closer look — returning a plain-English explanation of what it found and why.

Good to know

Frequently asked

Does WallUPI move real money?

No. WallUPI is a demonstration app — no real money moves. But the fraud detection underneath is real, the same logic that would protect you in a live payment.

Does it work without internet?

The first two layers of protection run entirely on your device, with no connection needed. The cloud second opinion only kicks in for borderline cases.

Why does being on a call matter?

It's one of the strongest signs of a live scam. Fraudsters stay on the line and keep talking so you don't have a moment to think — so WallUPI takes it seriously.

What happens at the critical level?

The payment is paused with a mandatory cooldown. That brief, enforced break is designed to defuse the pressure scammers rely on — giving you time to hang up and reconsider.

Who is WallUPI for?

It's a demonstration of what proactive fraud prevention could look like if it were built into payment apps by default — useful for showcases, research and education.
